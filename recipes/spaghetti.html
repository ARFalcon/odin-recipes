Spaghetti

Description

Your favorite spaghetti sauce gets a delicious make over when combined with cream cheese and cooked ground beef. It's mixed with hot cooked spaghetti for an easy supper.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 (24oz) jar spaghetti
  • 4 oz philadelphia cream cheese, cubed
  • 8 oz spaghetti, cooked and drained
  • 2 tablespoons Kraft grated parmesan

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti as directed on package
  2. Meanwhile, brown mean in large skillet. Stir in sauce and cream cheese; cook on low heat 3 to 5 min. or until sauce is well blended and heated through, stirring frequently
  3. Drain spaghetti. Add sauce; mix lightly. Place on platter; top with Parmesan
